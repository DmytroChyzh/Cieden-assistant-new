##  Role of the Design Strategist
Core message
At Cieden, designers are expected to think like product strategists.

What that means
understand business goals before proposing screens;
ask why a workflow exists, not just how it looks;
help prioritize features and scope;
spot friction that affects adoption or efficiency;
work closely with stakeholders and developers;
suggest improvements proactively.
Safe explanation
Our designers do more than shape the interface. They help clarify requirements, challenge assumptions, and make sure the product direction supports real user and business needs..

## Core Philosophy
- UX comes first because complex products succeed when users can complete tasks quickly, understand the logic, and trust the system.
- UI supports clarity and trust, but UX defines how the product works.
- Cieden approaches work as problem-solving, not decoration.
- The team uses research, analytics, validation, and structured product thinking to guide decisions.
- The goal is not just better screens, but better workflows, adoption, efficiency, and product outcomes.
